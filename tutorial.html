🔥 Fire TV Stick Setup Guide

⏱️ 5–10 minutes 📱 Our V12Mate Player ✅ Beginner friendly
V12Mate No Server Address Needed
⚠️ Very Important: If you already have Tivimate installed, please uninstall it first — otherwise V12mate will not install.
1
Part 1: Install Downloader App
1

Go to My Apps from Fire TV home screen.

2

Select Get more apps.

3

Choose Search, press Down, then OK. Type Downloader.

4

When results show, select Downloader and press OK.

5

Choose Downloader → Download and wait for it to install.

6

Do not open it yet. Press Back to return to the Home screen.

2
Part 2: Enable Developer Options & Unknown Sources
1

Go to Settings → My Fire TV.

2

Open About and click Fire TV Stick 7 times to enable Developer Options.

3

Press Back → Developer Options → enable ADB debugging.

4

Confirm ADB debugging is On.

5

Select Install Unknown Apps → give Downloader permission (press OK).

6

Press the Home button to return home.

💡 Tip: If Developer Options is hidden, go to About and click Fire TV Stick 7 times to enable it.
3
Part 3: Install V12Mate via Downloader
1

Open Downloader.

2

Press Allow to grant file access. Dismiss the welcome message (press OK).

3

Enter the Downloader code for V12Mate: 2494311 → press GO.

4

Accept Privacy Policy → Install → confirm Install again if asked.

5

Press Install → wait until installed. When complete, press OPEN.

6

Press Add playlist, then press V12MATE.

💡 If you only see "Cancel", your Tivimate is still installed. Uninstall it first.

7

Enter your Credentials (No Server Address needed — it's our player).

📋 Your Login Info (from email)

Username:Provided in your email
Password:Provided in your email
8

Press Next. Make sure to keep VOD and TV channels selected.

9

Wait for it to process (about 1 minute).

10

Press Done, wait a few seconds. You're ready to watch!

⚠️ If No Channels Load: Go to Settings → Playlists → V12Mate → Xtream Codes Parameters and double-check your username and password, including capital letters.

📅 EPG Note: The EPG (guide information) loads in the background. Please give it some time. You can view it in Settings → EPG.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is V12Mate, and how does it work on Firestick?
+
V12Mate is a media player app for IPTV on Firestick, organizing channels/playlists with an EPG (Electronic Program Guide). It requires a subscription from us.
Is V12Mate compatible with all Firestick models?
+
Yes, V12Mate works with most Firestick models. Ensure you have enough storage space and a solid Wi-Fi connection for optimal performance.
No content showing on V12Mate?
+
Delete or edit the playlist and re-add it — it may have been entered incorrectly. Please check your credentials carefully.
Troubleshooting tips?
+
Re-enable unknown sources/ADB if needed, clear cache if app is slow, reinstall for major issues, or use a VPN if your ISP blocks streaming.

Enter your portal configuration:

Login Required:Yes, activate this
Username:Your credentials
Password:Your credentials

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to register MAC address for Formuler?
+
No, Formuler boxes use Xtream Codes login method, so you only need username and password from your IPTV provider.
What's the difference between MyTVOnline 1 and 2?
+
MyTVOnline 2 is the newer version with improved interface and better Xtream Codes support. We recommend using MyTVOnline 2 for better performance.
Why won't my channels load?
+
Check that "Login Required" is enabled, verify your credentials are correct, ensure stable internet connection, and try restarting the MyTVOnline app.

📺 Smart TV Setup Guide — All Brands

⏱️ 5–7 minutes 📺 LG, Samsung, Sony & More ✅ App Store
Hot PlayerPerfect Player

Compatible: LG WebOS, Samsung Tizen, Sony Android TV, TCL, Hisense, Vizio, and other major brands

💡 Important: If your Smart TV has the Google Play Store, please follow the Android TV tutorial instead of this one.

On your Smart TV, open your App Store (LG Content Store, Samsung Apps, etc.).

5

Change Source to "Xtream Code" and fill in your details:

📋 Playlist Configuration

Playlist Name:Any name (e.g. "My IPTV")
Host:Your Server Address (from email)
Username:Your IPTV username
Password:Your IPTV password
6

Click "Save". Return to your Smart TV, click Refresh or restart the Hot Player app. Your IPTV channels should now load!

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Hot Player cost?
+
Hot Player costs $9 CAD for 1 year or $21 CAD for a lifetime license. The app itself doesn't include any channels or content — users need their own IPTV subscription.
Where do I find the MAC Address on my Smart TV?
+
The MAC Address is displayed in the Hot Player app under the "Account" section. It's automatically generated when you first open the app on your Smart TV.
Why are my channels not loading?
+
Check your internet connection, verify your IPTV credentials are correct, ensure your subscription is active, restart the Hot Player app, or contact your IPTV provider for support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I find the MAC Address on my Roku?
+
The MAC Address is displayed in the Hot Player app under the "Account" section. It's automatically generated when you first open the app on your Roku device.
What if Hot Player isn't available in my region?
+
Hot Player availability varies by region. You can try changing your Roku account region or use alternative IPTV apps like IBO Player Pro, GSE Smart IPTV, or other apps available in the Roku Channel Store.
Can I use multiple playlists on one Roku device?
+
Yes, Hot Player supports multiple playlists. You can add several playlists through the web interface at hotplayer.app and easily switch between them on your Roku device.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between XC API Login and MAC Server?
+
XC API Login uses username/password (Xtream Codes), while MAC Server uses your device's MAC address. XC API Login is more flexible and recommended for most users.
Can I use multiple IPTV services on BuzzTV?
+
Yes, you can configure multiple connections in Server Settings and switch between different IPTV providers easily from the home screen.
Why won't my channels load?
+
Verify your credentials are correct, ensure stable internet connection, check that you're using the correct connection method (XC API vs MAC), and try restarting the BuzzTV app.
