🔥 Fire TV Stick Setup Guide

⏱️ 5–10 minutes📱 Our V12Mate Player✅ Beginner friendly
V12MateNo Server Address Needed
⚠️ Very Important: If you already have Tivimate installed, please uninstall it first — otherwise V12mate will not install.
1
Part 1: Install Downloader App
1
Go to My Apps from Fire TV home screen.
2
Select Get more apps.
3
Choose Search, press Down, then OK. Type Downloader.
4
When results show, select Downloader and press OK.
5
Choose Downloader → Download.
6
Wait for it to download and install.
7
Do not open it yet. Press Back to the Home screen.
2
Part 2: Enable Developer Options & Unknown Sources
1
Go to Settings → My Fire TV.
2
Open About and click Fire TV Stick 7 times to enable Developer Options.
3
Press Back → Developer Options → enable ADB debugging.
4
Confirm ADB debugging is On.
5
Select Install Unknown Apps → give Downloader permission (press OK).
6
Press the Home button to return home.
3
Part 3: Install V12Mate via Downloader
1
Open Downloader.
2
Press Allow to grant file access.
3
Dismiss the welcome message (press OK).
4
Enter the Downloader code for V12Mate: 2494311 → press GO.
5
Accept Privacy Policy → Install → confirm if asked.
6
Press Install → wait until installed.
7
When complete, press OPEN.
⚠️ If you already have Tivimate installed, uninstall it first.
8
Press Add playlist.
9
Press V12MATE.
💡 If you only see "Cancel" — click here to fix it.
10
Enter your Credentials — No Server Address needed since it's our player.

📋 Your Login Info (received by email)

Username:Provided in your email
Password:Provided in your email
11
Press Next. Keep VOD and TV channels selected.
12
Wait for it to process (about 1 minute).
13
Press Done, wait a few seconds. 🎉 You're ready to watch!
⚠️ If No Channels Load: Go to Settings → Playlists → V12Mate → Xtream Codes Parameters and double-check your username and password including capital letters.
📅 EPG Note: Guide info loads in the background. View it in Settings → EPG.
💡 Tip: If Developer Options is hidden, go to About and click Fire TV Stick 7 times to enable it.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is V12Mate, and how does it work on Firestick?
+
V12Mate is a media player app for IPTV on Firestick, organizing channels/playlists with an EPG. It requires a subscription from us.
Is V12Mate compatible with all Firestick models?
+
Yes, V12Mate works with most Firestick models. Ensure you have enough storage and a solid Wi-Fi connection.
How do I add IPTV services to V12Mate?
+
Add your provider's Xtream Codes details in V12Mate's playlist section. You'll need username and password from IPTVV CANADA.
No content showing on V12Mate?
+
Delete or edit the playlist and re-add it — it may have been entered incorrectly. Please check your credentials.
Troubleshooting tips?
+
Re-enable unknown sources/ADB if needed, clear cache if app is slow, reinstall for major issues, or use a VPN if your ISP blocks streaming.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do I see "invalid" when logging in?
+
Double-check host/username/password. No extra spaces. URL must start with http:// or https://
What internet speed is recommended?
+
10 Mbps for SD, 25 Mbps for HD, 50+ Mbps for 4K. Test at fast.com
Can I cast to TV from my phone?
+
Yes, most IPTV apps support Chromecast. Look for the cast icon in the player. Screen mirroring also works on compatible TVs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where do I find the MAC Address?
+
The MAC Address is displayed in the Hot Player app under the "Account" section when you first open the app.
How much does Hot Player cost?
+
Hot Player costs $9 CAD for 1 year or $21 CAD for a lifetime license. It doesn't include channels — you need your own IPTV subscription.
What if Hot Player isn't available in my region?
+
Try changing your Roku account region, or use alternative apps like IBO Player Pro or GSE Smart IPTV from the Roku Channel Store.

Frequently Asked Questions

XC API Login vs MAC Server — difference?
+
XC API uses username/password (Xtream Codes). MAC Server uses your device's MAC address. XC API is more flexible and recommended.
Can I use multiple IPTV services on BuzzTV?
+
Yes. Configure multiple connections in Server Settings and switch between providers easily from the home screen.
Why won't my channels load?
+
Verify credentials, ensure stable internet, check connection method (XC API vs MAC), and try restarting the BuzzTV app.
